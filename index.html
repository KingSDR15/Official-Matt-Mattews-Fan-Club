Membership Plans

Supporter

$450 / month

Affordable way to support Matt and get the essentials — ideal for casual fans.

  • Early access to new singles (24–48 hours)
  • Monthly newsletter with behind-the-scenes notes
  • Access to members-only posts and polls
  • 10% early-bird discount on standard merch drops

Enthusiast

$612 / month

For active fans who want more content and closer access.

  • All Supporter benefits
  • Exclusive studio clips & demo previews (monthly)
  • Quarterly live Q&A (members-only)
  • Priority pre-sale link for tickets (no guaranteed seats)
  • 15% discount on merch, plus early access to limited drops

Premium

$832 / month

Deeper connection — merch, priority access, and small-group experiences.

  • All Enthusiast benefits
  • Signed limited-run merch (one item / year)
  • Exclusive backstage updates and photo galleries
  • Small-group virtual meet & greet (up to 12 people) — quarterly
  • Priority ticketing queue with notification and seat recommendations

Concert VIP

$1,075 / month

Designed for fans who want guaranteed, professional in-person access at shows.

  • All Premium benefits
  • One (1) Concert Meet & Greet per membership year:
    • Group photo with Matt (professional photographer or designated staff)
    • Exclusive 10–15 minute Q&A or acoustic snippet
    • Priority early entry and dedicated check-in
  • One (1) Soundcheck or VIP lounge access where available
  • First access to VIP ticket packages (subject to venue allocation)
  • 20% discount on premium merch

Private Patron

$1,500 / month

Ultra-limited. Small private experiences and concierge-level service (application required).

  • All Concert VIP benefits
  • Private meet & greet opportunities (max 6 people) — can include:
    • Private acoustic set or table-side meet up (where local laws & venue permit)
    • Individual photo and short video message
  • Dedicated concierge for ticketing and shipping of merch
  • Quarterly one-on-one virtual call (15–20 minutes) — scheduling required
  • Priority consideration for special appearances and limited events

Note: Private Patron experiences are capacity-limited and subject to scheduling, venue rules, and travel logistics. Final details confirmed after signup.

Membership perks, meet & greet inclusions, and scheduling are subject to availability, local venue rules, and applicable travel logistics. Meet & greet specifics (date, time, number of guests) will be confirmed via email after purchase.
